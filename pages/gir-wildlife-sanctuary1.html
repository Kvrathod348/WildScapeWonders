Welcome To Gir National Park

History

In the 19th century, the rulers of Indian princely states used to invite the British colonists for hunting expeditions. At the end of the 19th century, only about a dozen Asiatic lions were left in India, all of them in the Gir Forest, which was part of the Nawab of Junagarh's private hunting grounds. British viceroys brought the drastic decline of the lion population in Gir to the attention of the Nawab of Junagadh, who established the sanctuary.
Today, it is the only area in Asia where Asiatic lions occur and is considered one of the most important protected areas in Asia because of its biodiversity. The Gir ecosystem with its diverse flora and fauna is protected as a result of the efforts of the government forest department, wildlife activists and NGOs. It is now considered the jewel of Gujarat's ecological resources.
The Gir forest area and the Asiatic lions were first "protected" by the Nawab of the princely state of Junagadh at the start of the 20th century, which saved the lion population from being wiped out by trophy hunters. In 1965 the national park was established.

Best Time To Visit



Summer
28° To 45° Mar, Apr, May Jun
Lasting from March to May, summers in Gir are extremely humid and hot. The temperature ranges between 28 °C and 42 °C. The summer winds come with a gush of hot air and if you’re not used to it, it could be difficult to bear hence it is not the best time to visit Gir.Animals face the brunt of the heat too.


Winter
10° To 28° Nov, Dec, Jan, Feb
Winter is the best time to visit Gir for a family vacation. From November to February, the weather is pleasant and accommodating. It’s neither too hot nor too cold the temperature ranges from 10 °C to 22 °C. The Gir weather,combined with the lush greenery,makes this the best time to visit


Monsoon
23° To 34° Jul, Aug, Sep
While the animals get a much-needed respite from the sweltering heat during the monsoon months, they also prefer to stay sheltered from the rain. It is also impossible to conduct safaris. Due to the rains, Gir is closed from June to October hence, it is not the best time to visit Gir.for the upcoming winter season.
