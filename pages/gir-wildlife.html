Best Time To Visit



Summer
28° To 45° Mar, Apr, May Jun
Lasting from March to May, summers in Gir are extremely humid and hot. The temperature ranges between 28 °C and 42 °C. The summer winds come with a gush of hot air and if you’re not used to it, it could be difficult to bear hence it is not the best time to visit Gir.Animals face the brunt of the heat too.


Winter
10° To 28° Nov, Dec, Jan, Feb
Winter is the best time to visit Gir for a family vacation. From November to February, the weather is pleasant and accommodating. It’s neither too hot nor too cold the temperature ranges from 10 °C to 22 °C. The Gir weather,combined with the lush greenery,makes this the best time to visit


Monsoon
23° To 34° Jul, Aug, Sep
While the animals get a much-needed respite from the sweltering heat during the monsoon months, they also prefer to stay sheltered from the rain. It is also impossible to conduct safaris. Due to the rains, Gir is closed from June to October hence, it is not the best time to visit Gir.for the upcoming winter season.
